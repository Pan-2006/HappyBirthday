✦ A Birthday Blessing ✦
Two years since we first met,
and despite every hardship and difficulty, we made it work.
We are so different, yet so much the same —
and I wouldn’t give this up for anything.
It’s strange how we’ve had our differences...
we talk, we don’t talk, we’ve both walked away.
Yet somehow, we always find our way back to one another.
I truly thank God for that.
I believe it’s fate that keeps bringing us together,
and I’m not complaining. You are kind, crazy, and difficult to handle —
but that is exactly what I love about you.
Even when things are frustrating, as long as we support each other,
nothing else matters.
You make my days so much better.
Your voice, your laugh, your very presence...
to me, it is a gift.
I know I can be annoying and over the top,
teasing you until it’s frustrating — but that’s just how I love you.
I really do love you.
May this be just one of many birthdays we celebrate together.
To many more years, more birthdays, and more time together.
May this year carry you gently,
fill your heart with wonder,
and remind you how deeply you are loved.
" For I know the plans I have for you —
plans to prosper you and not to harm you,
plans to give you hope and a future. "
— Jeremiah 29:11
